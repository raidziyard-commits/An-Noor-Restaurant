Our Story

Panadura's Premier
Family Dining Destination

Welcome to An Noor Family Restaurant — where authentic Middle Eastern flavors meet the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality. Nestled at 39A Old Galle Road in the heart of Panadura, we have earned our reputation as one of the finest family dining spots in the region.

Under the gracious ownership of Mr. Rashad, our kitchen crafts each dish with love and tradition. From our signature Mandi Rice slow-cooked in a traditional tandoor, to our fragrant Biryani, sizzling Beef Cheese Koththu packed with generous beef pieces — every bite tells a story of authenticity.

Experience our exclusive Sawan & Majlis floor dining arrangements, perfect for family gatherings, celebrations, and corporate events. We invite you to sit back, relax, and let us take care of you.
